📐 Calculator Formulas

All formulas reference guide

📐
🎞️
Film Roll Calculator
KG ↔ Meter
Step 1 — KG per Meter
KG/Mtr = Width(m) × Micron(m) × Density × 1000
KG → Meter
Meter = KG ÷ KG/Mtr
Meter → KG
KG = Meter × KG/Mtr
🖨️
Printing GSM Calculator
Coating Weight → GSM
Sq. Meter
Sq.Mtr = Width(m) × Running Mtr
Coating Weight
Coating = Rewinder − Unwinder
📊 GSM Formula
GSM = (Coating × 1000) ÷ Sq.Mtr
🧪
Lamination GSM Calculator
Dam · OH · NCO → GSM
Sq. Meter
Sq.Mtr = Rubber(m) × Running Mtr
Net Adhesive
Net = (OH + NCO) − Dam
📊 GSM Formula
GSM = (Net × 1000) ÷ Sq.Mtr
⚖️ Ratio
Ratio = OH ÷ NCO
